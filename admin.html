SEO Settings (Search Engine Optimization)

Help your product appear in Google and other search engines

What: The title that appears in Google search results
Why: A good title gets more clicks from Google
How Long: 50-60 characters (currently: 0)
Example: "Essential Basic Phone - Simple, No Distractions"
Tip: Leave empty to use product name
What: The preview text under your title in Google
Why: Tells people what to expect before clicking
How Long: 150-160 characters (currently: 0)
Example: "Get back to basics with our simple phone. No internet, no apps, no distractions. Just reliable calls and texts. Perfect for digital detox."
Tip: Make it compelling to encourage clicks
What: Words people search for to find this product
Why: Helps Google understand what your product is
How: Separate with commas, use 5-10 keywords
Example: "basic phone, simple phone, dumb phone, no internet phone, minimalist phone"
Tip: Think like a customer - what would they search for?
What: The URL-friendly version of your product name
Why: Makes clean, readable URLs (yoursite.com/essential-basic-phone)
How: Use lowercase letters and hyphens only, no spaces
Example: "essential-basic-phone" or "classic-flip-phone-black"
Tip: Leave empty to auto-generate from product name
